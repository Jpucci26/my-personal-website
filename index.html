Sunny Bear facts

Named after the mountains she is from, Sunny is a Great Pyrenees Mountain Dog. I commonly refer to her as Sunny Bear and she has been my best for the last 16 months. I want to focus on just Sunny but if you would like to learn more about these dogs, please click on this link.

On your average day, you can find Sunny...
  • Snoozing
  • Eating
  • Enjoying a good bone
  • Being cute as hell
Those are her usual tendencies but when the weather is right, her favorite thing to do is play in the snow!
